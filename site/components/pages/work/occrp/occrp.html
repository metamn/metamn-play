Description

Organized Crime and Corruption Reporting Project helps citizens better understand how organized crime and corruption affect their lives. OCCRP runs in-depth investigative stories as well as the latest news about organized crime and corruption.

I was asked to redesign their website and support all screens from mobile devices up to large projectors

We've managed to come up with a modern, newspaper-like redesign with clean headlines and intuitive structure.
